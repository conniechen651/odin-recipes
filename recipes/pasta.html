Fusilli Alla Vodka With Basil and Parmesan

Description

This recipe is a spicy and creamy pasta. Using simple ingredients, you can quickly and easily make a decadent homemade vodka sauce that you can adjust to your liking. Paired with fresh basil, you will never get tired of this pasta!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 small garlic clove, finely grated
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons vodka
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper
  • 1 pound fusilli
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 ounce finely grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium. Add shallot and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 5 minutes
  2. Add tomato paste and cook, stirring occasionally, until paste is brick red and starts to caramelize, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add vodka and cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid is mostly evaporated, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add cream and red pepper flakes and stir until well blended. Season with salt and pepper, then remove from heat.
  5. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  6. Drain, reserving 1 cup pasta cooking liquid.
  7. Add pasta to skillet with sauce along with butter and 1/2 cup of pasta cooking liquid. Cook over medium low-heat, stirring constantly and adding more pasta cooking liquid as needed.
  8. Season with salt and pepper and add Parmesan, tossing to coat.
  9. Divide pasta among bowls, then top with basil and more parmesan.
