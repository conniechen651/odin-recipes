Garlic Chili Oil Noodles

Description

These garlic chili noodles are packed with flavor and perfect for a quick and easy meal. With ingredients fresher than instant ramen, but a cook time that is the same or even less than instant ramen, you will enjoy this recipe when you don't have much time to cook or just are feeling a bit lazy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Lao Gan Ma chili crisp oil
  • 2 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tsp Chinese black vinegar
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp white part of the scallions, sliced (save green parts)
  • 2 large garlic cloves, micro grated
  • 2 tsp ginger, optional
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 servings of noodles
  • 2 springs of cilantro, optional

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook noodles
  2. Add all other ingredients to a bowl except the cilantro and green parts of scallion.
  3. stir and then toss with your favorite noodles.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and green part of scallions.
